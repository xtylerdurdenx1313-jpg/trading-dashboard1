# Trading Strategy — Conservative Trend Follower (STARTER) ⚠️ WARNING: This is a default starter strategy. It is NOT proven or backtested. Replace this with your own strategy as soon as possible. This exists only to get you started. ## Rules 1. Only trade BTC and ETH — no altcoins until you customize this 2. Max leverage: 3x — no exceptions 3. Max risk per trade: 2% of capital — if your stop loss would lose more than 2%, reduce position size 4. Max open positions: 2 — one per asset max 5. Max drawdown: 10% — if total P&L drops below -10%, stop trading and alert me ## Entry Rules - Only enter LONG positions (no shorts with this starter strategy) - Entry signal: Price is above the 24h moving average AND the last 4 hours have been green (higher closes) - Confirmation: Volume in the last hour is above the 24h average volume - If both conditions are met, enter a long position at market price ## Exit Rules - Stop Loss: 3% below entry price — non-negotiable, always set this immediately - Take Profit: 6% above entry price (2:1 reward-to-risk) - Trailing Stop: If price moves 4% in your favor, move stop to breakeven (entry price) - Time Stop: If a position hasn't hit TP or SL after 48 hours, close it at market ## Position Sizing - Risk amount = 2% of current equity - Position size = Risk amount ÷ (Entry price × Stop loss %) - Apply leverage (max 3x) to the position size - Never risk more than 2% on a single trade ## What NOT To Do - Don't chase — if you missed the move, wait for the next one - Don't average down — if it's going against you, the stop loss handles it - Don't overtrade — "no setup" is a valid decision every spawn - Don't hold through major news events — close positions before known announcements
